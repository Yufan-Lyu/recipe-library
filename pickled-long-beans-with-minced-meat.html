Stir-Fried Pickled Long Beans
with Minced Meat

TIME15 minLEVELEasySERVES3–4

A fast and savory stir-fry with pickled long beans, browned minced meat, garlic, and oyster sauce.

PRODUCTS USED

Featured Products

INGREDIENTS

What You Need

Pickled long beans2 packs, about 162 g
Minced meat400–500 g
Pork or beefEither works
Garlic, minced4 cloves
Oyster sauce1 tsp
Sugar½ tsp
Cooking oil3–4 tbsp
Salt¼ tsp, optional
MSG¼–½ tsp, optional
ScallionsAs needed

METHOD

Start Cooking

  1. 01

    Prepare the garlic

    Finely mince 4 cloves of garlic. Open the two packs of pickled long beans and keep them ready to use. The packaged beans do not need to be rinsed or prepared.

  2. 02

    Cook the minced meat

    Heat 3–4 tablespoons cooking oil in a wok over medium-high heat. Add 400–500 g minced pork or beef. Break the meat apart quickly with a spatula. Stir-fry until fully cooked, lightly browned, and most of the liquid has evaporated. Add the minced garlic and continue cooking until fragrant.

  3. 03

    Add the pickled long beans and season

    Add the two packs of pickled long beans and stir-fry with the meat for 2 minutes. Add 1 teaspoon oyster sauce and ½ teaspoon sugar. Add ¼–½ teaspoon MSG if using. Stir-fry for another 30–60 seconds. Taste before adding salt. Add ¼ teaspoon salt only if needed. Finish with scallions and serve hot.

Kitchen tips

The packaged pickled long beans are ready to use. Pork or beef both work well. Cook the meat until lightly browned and most of its liquid is gone. Pickled long beans and oyster sauce already contain salt, so always taste before adding more.
